Bob and Tiffany: My Cats

Bob and Tiffany are brother and sister tabby cats. They were rescued from the streets at 6 months old. Bob is orange and Tiffany is white. They are 3 years old.

go to footer

Bob

Bob is a very playful cat. He loves to chase after his sister and nap on clean laundry. He is very friendly and loves to cuddle. He has a very loud purr and loves to be pet on the head.

Tiffany

Tiffany is a curious cat. She loves to explore the house at night and play catch with her toys. She loves butt pats and napping on chairs. She is very vocal and loves to meow for attention.

Top napping spots for Bob and Tiffany

  1. Cat Tree
  2. On my mother's keyboard
  3. Window Sill
  4. Couch
  5. Cat Beds
